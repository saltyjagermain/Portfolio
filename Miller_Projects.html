Android, Where's My Car?

September 19, 2017-October 5, 2017: Computer Science and Engineering

The idea for this project was to make an app using MIT AppInventor 2 while either following a template/tutorial or by coming up with your own idea. This project was called Android, Where's My Car? and it was a GPS app that saved your location that you could find directions to later. The Project Design Notebook shows my progress and some feedback from my classmates (not all of them helpful) And, the actual Downloadable Files contain 8 versions of the app (APK, AIA). I learned how to use SQL data bases, and how GPS works for an Android device. Some challeneges that I had to come over was the actual adding of ideas to the tutorial and coming up with the certain lines of code that I had to find by myself. My partner and I both brainstormed ideas that made the app more than just an app that remembers where you parked your car.
